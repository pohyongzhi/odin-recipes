Lasagna

Lasagna is an Italian dish with layers of pasta, meat or vegetables, cheese, and tomato sauce, baked until bubbly and golden.


To make lasagna, start by preparing a meat or vegetable sauce and a cheese mixture. Cook lasagna noodles until al dente. In a baking dish, layer the ingredients: start with a layer of sauce, then noodles, followed by cheese. Repeat the layers, finishing with a generous amount of cheese on top. Bake in the oven at 375째F (190째C) for about 45 minutes, until the top is golden and bubbly. Let it cool slightly before serving.


Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef or Italian sausage
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (28 ounces) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 can (6 ounces) tomato paste
  • 1 can (15 ounces) tomato sauce
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 2 teaspoons sugar
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil leaves
  • 1/2 teaspoon fennel seeds
  • 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
  • 1 tablespoon salt, divided
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 4 cups ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 3/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese, divided
  • 2 tablespoons fresh parsley, chopped
  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound mozzarella cheese, sliced or shredded

Steps

  1. Prepare the meat sauce by cooking ground beef or Italian sausage with onion and garlic. Add crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, tomato sauce, water, sugar, basil, fennel seeds, Italian seasoning, 1/2 tablespoon salt, and pepper. Simmer for 30 minutes.
  2. In a bowl, combine ricotta cheese, egg, 1/2 cup Parmesan cheese, parsley, and 1/2 tablespoon salt.
  3. Preheat the oven to 375째F (190째C).
  4. Cook lasagna noodles according to package instructions.
  5. Assemble the lasagna in a baking dish: spread a layer of meat sauce, followed by noodles, ricotta mixture, and mozzarella. Repeat the layers, finishing with mozzarella on top.
  6. Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes. Remove foil and bake for an additional 25 minutes, or until bubbly and golden.
  7. Let it cool for 15 minutes before serving.
